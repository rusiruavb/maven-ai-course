TechCorp Employee Handbook

Welcome to TechCorp! This handbook outlines our company policies, benefits, and culture.

VACATION POLICY
All full-time employees receive 15 days of paid vacation per year. Vacation days accrue monthly at a rate of 1.25 days per month. New employees can begin using vacation days after completing 90 days of employment. Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days. Vacation requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the employee portal.

SICK LEAVE
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. Sick leave does not roll over to the next year. If you need to take sick leave, please notify your manager as soon as possible. For absences longer than 3 consecutive days, a doctor's note may be required.

EMPLOYEE BENEFITS
TechCorp offers comprehensive benefits including:
- Health insurance (medical, dental, vision) with 80% employer contribution
- 401(k) retirement plan with 4% company match
- Life insurance coverage (2x annual salary)
- Flexible spending account (FSA) for healthcare and dependent care
- Employee assistance program (EAP) for counseling and support
- Gym membership reimbursement up to $50/month
- Professional development stipend of $1,500 per year
- Commuter benefits for public transportation

WORK HOURS AND FLEXIBILITY
Standard work hours are Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. We offer flexible work arrangements including remote work options. Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Core collaboration hours are 10 AM to 3 PM when all team members should be available.

PARENTAL LEAVE
New parents receive 12 weeks of paid parental leave. This applies to both birth and adoptive parents. Leave can be taken within the first year after the child's arrival.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
We encourage continuous learning and growth. Employees can attend conferences, workshops, and training programs. The annual professional development budget of $1,500 per employee can be used for courses, certifications, conferences, or books related to your role.

PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually in June and December. Reviews include self-assessment, manager evaluation, and peer feedback. Salary adjustments and promotions are typically considered during the December review cycle.

COMPANY CULTURE
TechCorp values innovation, collaboration, and work-life balance. We host monthly team-building activities, quarterly all-hands meetings, and annual company retreats. Our open-door policy encourages communication at all levels.

EQUIPMENT AND TECHNOLOGY
All employees receive a laptop, monitor, keyboard, and mouse. You can choose between Mac or Windows systems. Equipment is refreshed every 3 years. Home office stipend of $500 is provided for remote workers to set up their workspace.

CODE OF CONDUCT
We maintain a professional, respectful workplace. Harassment, discrimination, and unprofessional behavior are not tolerated. All employees must complete annual ethics and compliance training.
